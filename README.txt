== Description ==

### [SegMetrics](https://segmetrics.io) is the premier analytics platform for marketing professionals.

This plugin lets those of us using MemberMouse to bring in revenue, subscription and membership data into SegMetrics.

Get 100% clarity on where your leads come from, how they act, and how much your marketing is really worth.

_NOTE: You'll need the [SegMetrics plugin](https://wordpress.org/plugins/segmetrics/) to get started._

== Installation ==

In order to use the SegMetrics MemberMouse Add-On, you'll need both a SegMetrics account and the base [SegMetrics plugin](https://wordpress.org/plugins/segmetrics/).

### From within WordPress

1. Visit 'Plugins > Add New'
1. Search for 'SegMetrics' and install the plugin
1. Search for 'SegMetrics MemberMouse Add-On' and install the plugin
1. Activate SegMetrics and the MemberMouse Add-On from your Plugins page.
1. Go to "after activation" below.

### After activation

1. Find the `SegMetrics` item in your `settings` menu.
1. Follow the instructions to connect the plugin to your SegMetrics account
1. Log into SegMetrics, and go to your [Integrations](https://app.segmetrics.io/a/integrations/) page.
1. Find the MemberMouse integration, and click `connect`
1. Follow the instructions on the screen.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Does this plugin require a paid service? =

Yes, for it to work you must first have an account on SegMetrics.io

= I'm not seeing my subscribers. What gives? =

Make sure that your subscribers are being added to your email platform that you've connected with SegMetrics.
We only report on marketable leads in your email platform, so contacts must be added there to show up in SegMetrics.
